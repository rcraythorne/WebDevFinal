    What we used to catch fish in the picture above:


    1. Hairs Ears
      • We tied these ones ourselves
      • You can find them at any fly fishing store
    2. Prince Nymphs
      • You can get these at the fly fishing store just as you approach Duth John
    3. Zebra Midge
      • These must be ordered online. Make sure they have the heavy bead
    4. San Juan Worm
      • These can be bought, but they go quick!
